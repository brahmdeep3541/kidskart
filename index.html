Why Kids Kart?

In India, an estimated 26 millions of children are born every year. As per Census 2011, the share of children (0-6 years) accounts 13% of the total population in the Country. This indicates the huge scope of our product in future market.

Kids Kart focuses on expanding the share of wooden toys in the Indian toy market so that it will be the ultimate winner of Indian toy industry. It is a 5500 crore industry.

Why wooden toys ? :
Internationally the Chinese toys reached a value of US 17 billion dollars in 2021 also having a major share in the Indian toy industry while the Indian toy industry competes with the global competition .The Indian toy manufacturers are looking forward to takeover the market ending chinese supremacy by giving various incentives to the Indian toy industry including the launch of Koppal toy cluster which is operational from march 2022

Wooden toys market will get a boost due to this approach of government will ultimately overtake the international competition and plastic toy market helping our business to expand quickly
